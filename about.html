Our values

  • Scientific excellence: We strive to pursuit high research and innovation standards, conduct rigorous research and identify novel approaches that can make a significant positive impact in our society.
  • Integrity: We conduct research ethically, with honesty and transparency. We prioritize accurate reporting of data and research findings, respecting intellectual property and prioritizing the credibility of our work within the scientific community.
  • Team science: We promote and emphasize collaboration and collective effort to achieve our scientific goals. We leverage our diverse expertise and perspectives to tackle complex problems, fostering a culture of open communication and mutual respect.
  • Diversity: We embrace, value and respect our differences in backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences. We understand that fostering an inclusive environment where all voices are heard and respected enhances creativity and innovation.
  • Equity: Our work aims to achieve fairness in treatment, opportunities and access to high-quality healthcare for all individuals regardless of their background. We are actively involved in addressing existing biases and barriers through scientific advances that can improve health outcomes for all people.

Research environment

The MI2 is primarily located at the University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus – a modern education, research and patient care facility and one of the largest academic health centers in the west of the US. Built to promote multi-disciplinary collaborations, the campus includes a biomedical education area, a clinical area, and a research area with multiple centers and institutes that perform ground-breaking internationally renowned research. The MI2 also has members and space at Denver Health - a national reference community-serving medical institution.

Denver Metropolitan Area

Denver has been consistently ranked as one of the best cities to live by the US News and World Reports. Nicknamed the Mile High City for its 5,280-foot elevation, Denver is one of the sunniest cities in America (300 days of sunshine a year on average) and its location at the base of the Rocky Mountains provides a gateway to a great number of outdoor activities, including skiing, hiking, mountain biking and whitewater rafting. The area of Denver is also home to a flourishing arts scene and music is a big part of life for Denver citizens, with world class music venues such as the Red Rocks Amphitheater. During the last decade, Denver’s popularity has grown significantly, and it has become one of the fastest growing cities in the US.
