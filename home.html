Welcome to the website of the Medical Imaging and Machine Intelligence (MI2) initiative at the University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus.

Who We Are

The Medical Imaging and Machine Intelligence (MI2) initiative is a joint effort between scientist from the University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus, Children's Hospital Colorado and Denver Health. The MI2 initiative works towards the creation and clinical translation of innovative artifical intelligence technologies to improve the understanding, diagnosis and treatment of medical conditions, with priority on research that can enable a more objective, accurate and equitable delivery of healthcare services to all people. Our team includes experts in computer science, engineering, biostatistics, medicine, and radiology.
